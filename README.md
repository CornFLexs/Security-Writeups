## 🛠️ Strategic Methodology
My research focuses on identifying high-impact vulnerabilities that automated scanners often miss, aligning findings with **OWASP Top 10** and **MITRE ATT&CK** frameworks:

* **Business Logic & API Integrity:** I specialize in uncovering flaws in complex application workflows, such as insecure API implementations, broken object-level authorization (BOLA), and privilege escalation.
* **State & Lifecycle Analysis:** I conduct deep-dive assessments into how applications handle data across different states, including session persistence, account transitions, and data retention/deletion protocols.
* **Offensive Security Research:** Leveraging experience from law enforcement and independent testing, I simulate advanced threat actor techniques to identify critical entry points and data exposure risks.
* **Remediation & Risk Documentation:** I bridge the gap between technical exploitation and business risk, delivering actionable guidance and high-impact reports for stakeholders.
